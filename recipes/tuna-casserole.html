Tuna Casserole

This tuna casserole recipe is easy and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Egg noodles - Wide
  • Peas - Frozen
  • Tuna - Canned and drained
  • Cream of Mushroom
  • Cream of Chicken
  • Whole milk
  • Shredded cheese - Mexican or Cheddar
  • Dot's Pretzels - Crushed
  • Butter - Salted and melted
  • Salt and pepper - To taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Combine prepared noodles, peas, tuna, condensed soup, milk, and shredded cheese in a large pot or bowl. Stir well.
  2. Press noodle mixture into a buttered casserole dish.
  3. Mix together the crushed Dot's and melted butter and sprinkle over top of the casserole.
  4. Bake until bubbly and breadcrumbs are browned on top.
