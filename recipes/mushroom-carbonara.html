Mushroom Carbonara

A vegetarian carbonara with plenty of flavor!

Ingredients

  • Noodles - Preferably Orecchiette
  • Mushrooms - Cremini or button
  • Garlic cloves
  • Shallots
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Parsley
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper - To taste
